HEARTFIX

Mission

In a world full of distraction, we must fight hard to fix our minds and hearts on Jesus Christ. We have to focus on the Bible in the midst of chaos, fear, and trouble. The point of my music is to help others in their chaos and lead them to God's truth. Beginning with my upcoming instrumental album, Clean Heart, my music will feature a theme and scriptures to go along with that theme. Read the scripture, listen to the song, and let God fix your heart.

-Heartfix

"Pay attention and turn your ear to the sayings of the wise; apply your heart to what I teach, for it is pleasing when you keep them in your heart and have all of them ready on your lips. So that your trust may be in the Lord, I teach you today, even you."

-Proverbs 22:17-19 (NIV)
